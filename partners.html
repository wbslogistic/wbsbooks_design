Our partners

Waterstones

Waterstones' mission is to be the leading bookseller on the high street and online providing customers the widest choice, great value and expert advice from a team passionate about bookselling. Waterstones aims to interest and excite its customers and continually inspire people to read and engage in books.

Eurobooks

Eurobooks is a distinctive bookshop based in Bratislava specializing in Anglophone, French, German, Spanish and Slav original literature. The company is an independent importer providing an immediate and unlimited access to worldwide publishers.

Malé Centrum

Bookstores Malé Centrum and Publishing Centre were established in 1992. The company specializes in foreign literature, all fields of science and educational books and closely cooperates with more than two hundred specialist publishers around the world, including Britain, the United States, Germany, the Netherlands and Switzerland.

BOOKTRADING

The company BOOKTRADING has enjoyed many years of successful experience in book sales. Thanks to the team’s good communication strategy and creative solutions the company has built up solid business partnerships, working with all Bulgarian publishing houses and with many foreign ones.

FOLIO

FOLIO is a large book wholesaler company based in Czech Republic, specializing on foreign literature and offering a great range of books of all kinds.

BESTSELLERS

BESTSELLERS is the largest bookstore in Budapest that offers a wide range of English, French and German books of all kinds, as well as newspapers and magazines in foreign languages.

Ir Knygos

Academic and professional literature, lifestyle and training (learning) book. Main topics: social sciences, humanities, medicine, art and architecture. In the areas of language and books orders.

Ruskitabevi

Russian bookstore pleased to offer books in Russian for adults and children! You will find news of modern fiction, as well as favorite childhood poems and stories, interesting stories, educational history, educational benefits for your children.
